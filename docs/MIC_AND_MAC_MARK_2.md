# **The Idea :-**

Mic And Mac Glow Bars helps urban Indians stay fresh, clean, and confident from home to work, even through pollution, heat, and daily transit fatigue.

# 

# **The Purpose :-**

* India has pollution  
* People travel from home to work in heat, dust, sweat, traffic, and fatigue  
* By morning transit or office arrival, many people feel dull, tired, and visually drained  
* The body is present, but the freshness is gone


# **The Position :-**

Glow Bar should not be positioned as a bathing product.

It should be positioned as a morning freshness solution for transit fatigue and urban pollution.

# **The Message :-**

The customer is not just buying soap.

The customer is buying \- \[ glowing skin | fresh appearance | clean feeling | morning confidence | protection from urban dullness \]

# **Customer Psychology :-**

* I do not want to look tired in the morning.  
* I want to feel fresh before office.  
* I want a clean start despite pollution.  
* I want a product that matches urban life.

# **The Selling Point :-**

* Freshness after bathing  
* Confidence before work  
* Protection against urban dullness  
* The better daily beginning
